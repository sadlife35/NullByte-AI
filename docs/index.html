Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of data can I generate with Nullbyte AI?

Nullbyte AI can generate a wide range of structured and semi-structured data, from simple tabular datasets to complex multi-table relational schemas. You can define data types, relationships, and even inject specific edge cases based on text prompts or by uploading existing schema information.

How does Nullbyte AI handle data privacy and compliance?

Privacy is at our core. We offer advanced PII (Personally Identifiable Information) handling, with a specific focus on India's DPDP Act. Our roadmap includes Differential Privacy for even stronger guarantees. We also provide tools for bias detection and ethical AI scorecards.

Is there a free tier available?

Yes, we plan to offer a Free Tier / Community plan that provides access to core generation features with some usage limits. It's perfect for exploring Nullbyte AI and for small projects. Check our conceptual pricing for more details.

Who is Nullbyte AI for?

Nullbyte AI is designed for solo developers, lean startup teams, enterprises, researchers, and data scientists who need high-quality, privacy-aware synthetic data to accelerate development, testing, and research without compromising on data quality or privacy.

What makes Nullbyte AI different?

Our key differentiators include a strong India-first approach with DPDP compliance and local language understanding, advanced out-of-the-box privacy and bias tools, a developer/startup-friendly workflow, and a vision for a community-driven platform with explainability at its heart.
